Forest Treehouse

Rila Forest

Live among the trees in this spectacular treehouse built 20 feet above ground. This architectural marvel combines eco-friendly design with luxury, featuring large windows that bring the forest inside. Wake up to bird songs and fall asleep to the gentle rustling of leaves.

Activities

  • Guided nature walks
  • Bird watching tours
  • Outdoor yoga sessions
  • Stargazing nights

Amenities

  • Solar-powered electricity
  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • Compostable toilets
  • Eco-friendly toiletries

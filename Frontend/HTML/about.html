About Beyond the City

Connecting people with nature through sustainable rural tourism

Our Mission

Beyond the City was born from a simple belief: that authentic travel experiences lie beyond the bustling streets of urban centers. We're dedicated to showcasing the beauty, tranquility, and cultural richness of rural destinations while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve natural environments.

Our platform connects conscious travelers with carefully curated eco-friendly accommodations, from mountain cabins to organic farms, each offering a unique opportunity to disconnect from the digital world and reconnect with nature, local traditions, and yourself.

Our Story

Beyond the City started in 2022 when our founders, exhausted from city life and conventional tourism, embarked on a journey to discover hidden rural gems. What they found was transformative: welcoming farming communities, breathtaking natural landscapes, and hosts passionate about sharing their way of life sustainably.

They realized that many people craved these authentic experiences but didn't know where to find them. Thus, Beyond the City was created to bridge this gap, making rural and eco-tourism accessible while ensuring that it benefits both travelers and local communities.

Today, we work with over 200 carefully selected properties across diverse rural regions, each committed to sustainable practices and authentic hospitality. We're not just a booking platform – we're a community of travelers and hosts united by a love for nature and responsible tourism.
